Bring Your Treasure Home

How to Play

  1. Use the left or right arrow key to highlight a character. Press spacebar to select.
  2. Use the left, right, up or down arrow keys to move your character toward the ship.
  3. If you hit a bug, you lose a life! When you reach 0 lives, the game ends.
  4. Sometimes treasure will appear, pick up the treasure before returning to the ship for extra points.
  5. See how many times you can make it back to your ship! Good luck!
