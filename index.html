ESCAPE the Bug Invasion

  • SCORE: 0
  • LIVES: ❤❤❤

HOW TO PLAY🕹

  1. Use the ⬅️, ➡️, ⬆️ or ⬇️ arrow keys to move your character toward the ship.
  2. Move to the water 🌊 to escape the bugs.
  3. If you hit a bug 🐞, you lose a life! When you reach 0 lives left and get hit again, the game ends.
  4. See how many times you can make it to the water! Good luck! 👍
